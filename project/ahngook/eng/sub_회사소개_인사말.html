Greetings

Ahn-gook Pharm has endeavored to develop and supply excellent medicines for public health
for the past 60 years with a mission of
“promoting human health and happiness through development of excellent medicines.”
Thanks to such continuous efforts and growth,
Ahn-gook Pharm was listed on KOSDAQ, practices transparent management,
and is positioned to join the top tier of the industry.
Now, Ahn-gook Pharm will make a second leap to implement the ‘AHN-GOOK 2030 NEW VISION.’

Provide safer and differentiated health care
by combining medical technologies and digital information

Today, we face a world where only the companies with digital-based technologies and capabilities for the 4th Industrial Revolution can survive.
To cope with this, Ahn-gook Pharm focuses on recruitment and development of human resources with digital marketing capabilities
to secure core technologies through development of total healthcare products
and services and to grow as a K-Health company, based on the 5 strategic directions -
Differentiation, Global, Ethical management, People management, and Digital.

Greetings Our Mission

Become a K-Health company that improves the quality of life
through comprehensive healthcare services

In line with the today’s healthcare needs, we will practice evidence/academy-based sales and marketing and promote differentiated new drug development that improves the quality of life in this era of 100-year lifespan. Our biomedicine business will make advancements in the development of antibody drugs and open innovation, and the Digital business will provide total healthcare products and services that meet the needs of today’s ever-changing market to continue growing as a K-Health company. We will adapt to the fast-changing world using our 60 years of knowhow and take on challenges with openness.

Ahn-gook Pharm has grown and developed through your support, and in return, our ongoing research and development efforts will continue to find ways to enhance your health and quality of life. We ask for your continued interest and encouragement and will listen openly to your concerns. We hope you find useful information here.
