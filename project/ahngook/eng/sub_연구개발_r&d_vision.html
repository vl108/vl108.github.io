R&D VISION.

Based on its 60-year pharmaceutical heritage and experience since its foundation in 1959, Ahn-gook Pharm has expanded the company’s specialties to the respiratory system, digestive system, and circulatory system, and has actively performed R&D in these areas.
Ahn-gook Pharm is committed to continued growth as a leader of the pharmaceutical industry in the 21st century.

DRL

Developmental Resources Leverage

In particular, Ahn-gook Pharm adopts the Developmental Resources Leverage (DRL) strategy in which it leverages
the most useful and effective development resources as growth axes to drive expansion.
